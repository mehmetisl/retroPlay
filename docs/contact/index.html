Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer internships?

Yes! We offer internships for students and recent graduates in game development, art, sound design, and marketing. Check our careers page for current opportunities.

Where can I buy your games?

Our games are available on Steam, Nintendo eShop, PlayStation Store, Xbox Marketplace, and our own website. Some mobile titles are available on iOS App Store and Google Play.

Do you accept game ideas from fans?

While we love hearing from our community, we cannot accept or review unsolicited game ideas for legal reasons. We encourage you to share feedback on our existing games instead!

How can I report a bug in a game?

Please visit our support page for the specific game and submit a bug report through our ticketing system. Include as much detail as possible to help us reproduce and fix the issue.
