About the Game

Dungeon Bytes is a challenging roguelike that combines pixel art aesthetics with deep, modern gameplay systems. Descend into an ever-changing dungeon filled with monsters, traps, and treasures. Choose from 8 distinct character classes, each with unique abilities and progression paths. No two runs are ever the same thanks to procedural generation and over 500 items, weapons, and spells to discover. Permadeath raises the stakes, but each failure teaches you something new about the dungeon's secrets.

Features

  • Procedurally generated dungeons ensure no two runs are the same
  • 8 unique character classes with distinct playstyles
  • Over 500 items, weapons, and spells to discover
  • Challenging permadeath mechanics with persistent progression
  • Daily challenges and leaderboards
