About the Game

Highway Hustler combines the visual charm of 16-bit era racing games with sophisticated modern physics and handling. Race through 20 meticulously designed tracks across 5 distinct environments, from neon-lit cities to dusty desert roads. Unlock and customize 30 unique vehicles, each with their own handling characteristics and upgrade paths. Challenge friends in local split-screen multiplayer or compete online in tournaments and seasonal events.

Features

  • AI opponents
  • Customizable vehicles
  • Multiplayer for up to 4 players
  • Seasonal events
