About Retro Play

We're a team of passionate developers, artists, and gamers dedicated to creating authentic retro gaming experiences enhanced by modern technology.

Our Story

Retro Play was founded in 2018 by a group of game developers who grew up during the golden age of arcade and console gaming. Bonded by a shared nostalgia for pixel art, chiptunes, and the pure gameplay of classic titles, we set out to create games that capture that magic while leveraging modern technology.

What started as a small indie studio has grown into a team of 15 dedicated professionals, all united by our mission to bridge the gap between retro and modern gaming experiences.

Each game we create is a labor of love, designed to evoke the feelings we had when we first discovered gaming, while offering the depth, accessibility, and polish that today's players expect.

Our Mission

Authentic Retro Feel

We meticulously craft every pixel, sound effect, and gameplay mechanic to deliver an authentic retro gaming experience that feels like a beloved classic.

Modern Technology

We leverage cutting-edge development tools and platforms to ensure our games run smoothly across devices while adding features that weren't possible in the original era.

Innovation Through Nostalgia

We don't just recreate the past—we reimagine it. Our games introduce innovative mechanics and storytelling while maintaining the core appeal of retro gaming.
